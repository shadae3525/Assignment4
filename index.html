Who is George Boole

George Boole was an English mathematician who helped establish modern symbolic logic. His algebra of logic is now called Boolean algebra. It is basic to the design of digital computer circuits. Mr. Boole was born on November 2, 1815, Lincoln, Lincolnshire, England. He died December 8th, 1864, Ballintemple, County Cork, Ireland. Boole was given his first mathematics lesson by his father, a tradesman, who also taught him in mathematics. Aside from his father's help, Boole was self taught in mathematics.
